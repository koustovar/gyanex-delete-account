01

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Gyanex account directly from the app or by contacting our support team via email.

1
Open the Gyanex app and log in to your account.
2
Go to Profile › Settings.
3
Tap "Security" from the settings menu.
4
Confirm deletion when prompted to finalize the request.
OR

Send us an email and our team will process your deletion request within 2 business days.

Subject line required:

"Account Deletion Request"

Include your registered email address in the message body.

support.gyanex@gmail.com
02

What Data Gets Deleted?

Upon a confirmed deletion request, the following data associated with your account will be permanently removed:

Profile Information Name, email, profile picture
Class & Assignment Data Classes, assignments, activity history
Purchase Records Subscription & payment history
Firebase Account Data All associated backend records
⚠ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once confirmed, your data cannot be recovered, and access to Gyanex will be revoked immediately.
